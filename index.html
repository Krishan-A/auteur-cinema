Auteur Cinema

Movies are created through hard work, long hours, and intense collaboration by many filmmakers, however the films by these directors are unique because:

  • The films that compose their bodies of work are immediately identifiable as theirs
  • Their style is communicates across many aspects of the creative process
  • Recognizable filmmaking elements remain consistent even as collaborators change
